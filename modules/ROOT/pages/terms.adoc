= Definition of Terms

[width="100%",cols="1,3",options="header",]
|===
|Term |Description
|OBC |SC-OBC Module V1.
|Dev Board |SC-OBC Module V1 Development Board.
|Versal |AMD Versal AI Edge Series VE2302, the main processor of the OBC.
|Versal PMC MIO 0 |Multiplexed I/O in Bank 500 of the Versal PS.
|Versal PMC MIO 1 |Multiplexed I/O in Bank 501 of the Versal PS.
|Versal LPD MIO |Multiplexed I/O in Bank 502 of the Versal PS.
|Versal HDIO |Low-speed I/O in Bank 302 of the Versal PL.
|Versal XPIO |High-speed I/O in Bank 703 of the Versal PL.
|IGLOO2 |Microchip IGLOO2 FPGA, the safety processor of the OBC.
|IGLOO2 MSIO (Bank 5) |Low-speed or high-speed multi-standard I/O in Bank 5 of the IGLOO2.
|IGLOO2 MSIO (Bank 8) |Low-speed or high-speed multi-standard I/O in Bank 8 of the IGLOO2.
|Main power supply |For the OBC, +5V supplied from the Dev Board. +
For the Dev Board, +12V supplied from the AC adapter.
|FTDI IC |FT4232H-56Q, a quad high-speed USB to multipurpose UART/MPSSE IC manufactured by FTDI.
|===
